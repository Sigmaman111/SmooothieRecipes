Ingredients

  • 1 romaine lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing
  • Grilled chicken (optional)

Steps

  1. Chop lettuce and add to bowl.
  2. Add croutons and parmesan.
  3. Toss with Caesar dressing.
  4. Top with grilled chicken if desired.
