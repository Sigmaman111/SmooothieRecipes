Strawberry & Oat Smoothie Bowl

Prep Time: 5 minutes • Cook Time: 0 minutes

My first smoothie bowl recipe, good to have as breakfast.

Ingredients

  • ¾ of a medium banana, frozen
  • 8 frozen strawberries
  • 60 ml milk (¼ cup)
  • 2 tbsp Greek yoghurt
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  • ½ teaspoon chia seeds (optional)

Method

  1. Add milk into blender or bowl.
  2. Add strawberries and banana to the same blender or bowl.
  3. Add Greek yoghurt and honey.
  4. Blend using a jug or stick blender until smooth.
  5. Pour into a bowl and top with fruit, nuts, or granola if desired.
