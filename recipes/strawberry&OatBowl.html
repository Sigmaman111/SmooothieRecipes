Strawberry & oat Smoothy bowl

← Back to Cookbook

Ingredients

  • ¾ of a medium banana, frozen
  • 8 frozen strawberries
  • 60 ml milk (¼ cup)
  • 2 tbsp Greek yoghurt
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  • ½teaspoon chia seeds (optional)

Method

  1. Add milk into blender/bowl.
  2. Add strawberries and banana to same blender/bowl.
  3. Add greek yoghurt and honey.
  4. Blend in jug blender of using stick blender.
